At ZIT, my first project called "Our Proud" celebrates the national anthem.
Using only HTML, we created a simple yet impactful experience.
The project includes audio of the anthem and images that evoke patriotism.
It's a tribute to our nation's pride and unity, showcasing the power of
simplicity in digital storytelling. With "Our Proud," we aim to inspire
a sense of belonging and appreciation for our heritage.

For my recent project, I made a Premium League table using HTML and CSS.
It shows stats for ten top teams, like their rank, matches played, wins, draws,
and goals against. At the top, you'll see the Premium League logo. It's easy to
use and helps fans see how well their favorite teams are doing in the league. With
clean formatting and clear organization, this table provides a straightforward
way for fans to stay updated on the latest standings and performances. Whether
you're a die-hard supporter or just curious about the league, this project offers
a user-friendly platform to engage with the exciting world of football.

At the Orange Digital Center, my team and I embarked on an exhilarating project to
create a captivating 2D running game using JavaScript and Node.js. Drawing inspiration
from classic platformers, we combined our creativity and technical expertise to develop
a dynamic gaming experience that would delight players of all ages. Our game featured vibrant
graphics, smooth animations, and immersive sound effects, transporting players to a thrilling world filled with
obstacles and challenges. Leveraging the power of Node.js, we implemented real-time
multiplayer functionality, allowing users to compete against friends or team
up for cooperative gameplay. Throughout the development process, we collaborated closely,
brainstorming ideas, refining mechanics, and overcoming obstacles together. With each line of code and iteration, we pushed the
boundaries of what was possible, striving to deliver a polished and engaging gaming experience.
In the end, our hard work paid off as we unveiled our creation to the world.
From the adrenaline-pumping gameplay to the seamless multiplayer integration, our 2D running
game captivated players and showcased the immense potential of JavaScript
and Node.js in game development. It was a testament to the power of teamwork, creativity, and
innovation, and a project that we were proud to have accomplished at the Orange Digital Center.
